Privacy policy

What we collect: If you request 1:1 coaching, we collect your email address. We do not require your name.

Why we collect it: To coordinate the 1:1 coaching request and (optionally) follow up with important updates about safety, docs, or breaking changes.

How it's processed: The funnel form submits via a third-party form/mail service (lfbuilders API). Your email and the form metadata are transmitted to that provider to deliver the message.
