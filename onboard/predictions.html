Overview

gary-predictions-v1 is a new decision layer that aims to turn chaos into clarity. Think: fewer random entries, more consistent criteria, and a brutally clear explanation when it says "no."

  • ✓

    One answer you can act on

    Converts raw market + chain signals into a bounded decision: buy / wait / skip, plus confidence and risk.

  • ✓

    Explainable by design

    Every call includes a short "why" so you can tune settings with intent, not superstition.

  • ✓

    Hard gates stay hard

    Predictions don't override safety. The engine still enforces route quality, liquidity, data readiness, and constraints.

Why it's different

It's opinionated: defaults are conservative; it's designed to skip aggressively when signals are weak.
It's execution-aware: it cares about route friction and failure modes, not just "number go up."
It's aiming for elite: built to be the best memecoin trading assistant in the game - without claiming magic.

We're building it in public and rolling out access carefully while we validate data quality, stability, and failure modes.

Capabilities

This isn't a "chat bot." It's a decision engine: real-time signals → bounded outputs → auditable reasons.

  • ✓

    Realtime market read

    Tracks fast-changing leader dynamics and reacts to momentum shifts as they happen.

  • ✓

  "reason": "short explanation"

Outputs are bounded on purpose.

The goal is consistency: clear answers, not vibes.

Model

gary-predictions-v1 is a structured predictions model that consumes compact signals and returns a bounded JSON decision. The goal is generalization: any mint + current data → buy / wait / skip guidance for entries.

  • 1

    Inputs

    Market regime, liquidity/volume, momentum, route friction, and anomaly/rug signals (no private keys).

  • 2

    Outputs

    Decision + confidence + risk + short explanation. Designed to be auditable and actionable.

  • 3

    Safety posture

    Predictions do not override hard gates. They inform entries; they do not force them.
